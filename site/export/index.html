Bike Walk Vote PAC is the political voice for Oregonians who bike, walk, or take transit. We work to elect candidates who will best support transportation choices and livable communities.

Volunteer

Bike Walk Vote is a 100% grassroots, volunteer-driven organization. Everything we do depends on people like yourself volunteering: everything from knocking on doors and making phone calls to organizing events and lobbying legislators. If you're interested in helping, contact Volunteer Coordinator Beth Flanagan by emailing us at beth@bikewalkvote.org .
